music

I'd dreamed of playing electric guitar for as long as I could remember. During the summer of 2017, when I was 16 years old, I turned that dream into a reality when I bought my first electric guitar with my own money.

I've always had an ear for music; I loved playing piano by ear, and my band instructor later told me I had perfect pitch.

Reading music was never my thing. I preferred to cut out the middleman and just play... so that's what I did.

It's what I still do today, and I love doing it.

My passion lies in rock and metal instrumentals.
